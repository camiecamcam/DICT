Who We Are

The Department of Information and Communications Technology of Ilocos Sur (DICT Ilocos Sur) shall be the

primary policy, planning, coordinating, implementing, and administrative entity

that will oversee, develop, and promote ICT infrastructure and digital innovation

to support the province's growth and modernization.
