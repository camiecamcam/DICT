Cybercrime Investigation and Coordinating Center

The Cybercrime Investigation and Coordinating Center (CICC), which was created upon the approval of Republic Act 10175 or the Cybercrime Prevention Act of 2012,
is an attached agency of the Department of Information and Communications Technology (DICT), and is chaired by the DICT Secretary.
